Our Story



After 36 straight hours of work
and many power naps on the futon,
FluxCache came to life.



More than half of Americans have less than
three months’ worth of emergency savings,
according to a survey conducted by
personal finance website Bankrate.


In grappling today’s current climate,
emergency savings are unnervingly
one step away from being depleted.
FLuxCache is the simple and reliable
solution to financial security and establishing
life stability in our unstable world.
